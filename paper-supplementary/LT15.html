Supplementary Material for LT 15 Paper

This page provides supplementary material for the paper Automatic Extraction of Session-Based Workload Specifications for Architecure-Level Performance Models (under review), which introduces the WESSBAS transformation to Palladio Component Models.

WESSBAS is an acronym for Workload Extraction and Specification for Session-Based Application Systems.
